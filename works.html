Works

Four material and spatial investigations are listed as archive entries, with image positions reserved for documentation.

  1. 01 Land of Fish and Rice Year: 2024 Type: Installation / photographic documentation / domestic investigation Medium: Food residue, rice grains, fish bones, plastic mesh bags, price labels, QR-code labels, domestic space, photography Domestic leftovers are treated as forensic evidence to reveal invisible tensions and power relations within the family structure.
  2. 02 Arch / Column Year: 2024 Type: Installation / spatial work / architectural intervention Medium: Doric columns, denim, red doormat, locks, bells, spatial arrangement Architectural symbols of authority are transformed into bodily thresholds, using columns, denim, and entry gestures to question power, masculinity, and passage.
  3. 03 Synthetic Diamond Year: 2024 Type: Jewelry / object / symbolic material study Medium: Metal, tablet-like form, belt buckle, ring A pharmaceutical tablet is reframed as a synthetic gem, questioning value, hardness, desire, and symbolic legitimacy.
  4. 04 Gewu / Investigation Year: 2023–2024 Type: Jewelry / object-based research / measurement system Medium: Laser-cut stainless steel, arc welding, dried fruit, engineering drawing logic Engineering drawing and measurement systems are transformed into physical jewelry frames, questioning whether measurement is neutral or subjective.
