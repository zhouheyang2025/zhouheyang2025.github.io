## Main projects

### 01 Land of Fish and Rice

Chinese title: 鱼米之乡

Core idea:
Domestic leftovers are treated as forensic evidence to reveal invisible tensions and power relations within the family structure.

Materials / media:
food residue, rice grains, fish bones, plastic mesh bags, price labels, QR-code labels, domestic space, photography

Tone:
forensic, domestic, cold, quiet, evidential

Page direction:
The page should feel like a domestic investigation file.

Possible sections:
- overview
- process
- evidence labels
- weighing and classification
- restored scene
- documentation images

### 02 Gewu / Investigation

Core idea:
Engineering drawing and measurement systems are transformed into physical jewelry frames, questioning whether measurement is neutral or subjective.

Materials / media:
laser-cut stainless steel, arc welding, dried fruit, jewelry objects, measurement symbols, drawing logic

Tone:
precise, analytical, controlled, incomplete

Page direction:
The page should combine object photography with diagrams, measurement marks, and material details.

### 03 Synthetic Diamond

Core idea:
A pharmaceutical tablet is reframed as a synthetic gem, questioning value systems, hardness, desire, and symbolic legitimacy.

Materials / media:
metal, tablet-like form, belt buckle, ring, symbolic geometry

Tone:
critical, witty, restrained, artificial

Page direction:
The page should not look like a product page. It should look like a material-symbol investigation.

### 04 Arch / Column

Core idea:
Architectural symbols of authority are transformed into bodily thresholds, using columns, denim, and entry gestures to question power, masculinity, and passage.

Materials / media:
Doric columns, denim, red doormat, locks, bells, installation space

Tone:
spatial, public, bodily, architectural, slightly absurd

Page direction:
The page should emphasize scale, passage, human body, and threshold.
