Project file / domestic investigation

01

Land of Fish and Rice

Domestic leftovers are treated as forensic evidence to reveal invisible tensions and power relations within the family structure.

Chinese title
鱼米之乡
Year
2024
Medium
Food residue, rice grains, fish bones, plastic mesh bags, price labels, QR-code labels, domestic space, photography
Material
Domestic leftovers, disposable kitchen strainers, printed labels, household objects
Type
Installation / photographic documentation / domestic investigation
Size
Variable

01-05

Investigation sequence

  1. 01

    Overview

    Land of Fish and Rice begins with residues left after family meals. Rice grains, fish bones, and other remains are collected, classified, weighed, and marked like evidence. The work transforms domestic waste into a system of observation.

  2. 02

    Evidence collection

    Leftovers are marked directly on plates with tags that borrow the language of evidence. The meal becomes a record of gestures, decisions, and unfinished domestic negotiation.

  3. 03

    Classification and weighing

    Rice grains, fish bones, and other traces are washed, separated, bagged in disposable kitchen strainers, and weighed individually. The process gives fragile remains a controlled archival structure.

  4. 04

    Price-label coding

    Supermarket-style price codes and QR-code labels are applied to the collected residues. Domestic waste is translated into a system of value, quantity, and coded circulation.

  5. 05

    Restored domestic scene

    The coded objects return to corners of the home, where they accumulate as quiet traces of unresolved domestic speech.
